# Global-AI-Adoption-and-Business-Performance-Dashboard

# Introduction

This project analyse the impact of Artificial Interlligence (AI) on business performance across variouse industries  using Excel and Power BI. The analysis provide important insights to help organizations make informed decisions regarding AI implimentationa nd business strategy.

# Dataset Information

Source: Kaggle 

# Business Problem

-How does Al impact to company revenue growth?
-Which industries have the highest and lowest AI adoption rate?
-How does AI impact to workforce changers?
-How can organizations use AL insights to improve their business performance and startegic deision making?
-What are the key trends in Al across industries?

# Data Cleaning

Tools Used: Microsoft Excel
-Removed Duplicates
-Checked missing values
-Validated data for accuracy and completeness

# Data Visualization

Tools Used:Microsoft Power BI

# Key Findings

-Global AI adoption across industries has increased significantly.

-Gaming, Education, and Manufacturing are the top three industries with the highest AI adoption rates. 

-Manufacturing, Automobile, and Legal are the top three industries experiencing the highest levels of AI-related job loss.

-AI adoption has significantly increased revenue.

-AI adoption has also contributed to increased consumer trust.

-The United Kingdom (UK) has the highest AI adoption rate among the countries analyzed in this study
